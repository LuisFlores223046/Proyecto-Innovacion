\begin{DoxyVerb}Convert the text form of a TTL to an integer.

The BIND 8 units syntax for TTLs (e.g. '1w6d4h3m10s') is supported.

*text*, a ``str``, the textual TTL.

Raises ``dns.ttl.BadTTL`` if the TTL is not well-formed.

Returns an ``int``.
