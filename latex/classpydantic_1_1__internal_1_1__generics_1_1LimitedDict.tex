\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\begin{DoxyVerb}Limit the size/length of a dict used for caching to avoid unlimited increase in memory usage.

Since the dict is ordered, and we always remove elements from the beginning, this is effectively a FIFO cache.
\end{DoxyVerb}
